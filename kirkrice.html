About Kirk Rice LLP

More Than Numbers — A True Partnership

Based at Victoria House in Fleet, Hampshire, Kirk Rice LLP is a full-service chartered accountancy and financial advisory practice. We serve growing businesses, entrepreneurs, and individuals who expect more than compliance — they expect clarity, strategy, and genuine care.

  • 🎯

    Precision You Can Rely On

    We believe accuracy is non-negotiable. Every figure, filing, and financial strategy is handled with meticulous attention to detail.

  • 🤝

    Long-Term Client Relationships

    Our clients don't just stay — they thrive. With partnerships spanning over a decade, we're invested in your continued success.

  • 🌱

    Proactive, Not Reactive

    From HMRC readiness to tax planning, we anticipate challenges before they arise — so you're always a step ahead.
